# ADC Mailing List Rules

Welcome to the ADC mailing list.

This list is used for discussion, announcements, and development of ADC Archiver.

## 1. General Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Stay on topic

## 2. Subject Prefixes

To keep the list organized, always use a subject prefix:

- [ANN] Announcements and releases (core maintainers only)
- [BUG] Bug reports
- [PATCH] Patch proposals
- [DEV] Development discussion
- [DISCUSS] General discussion
- [RFC] Proposals and design ideas
- [HELP] Questions and user support
- [MISC] Miscellaneous

Messages without a prefix may be ignored or corrected.

## 3. Announcements

- [ANN] posts are reserved for maintainers
- Keep announcements clear and informative
- Avoid unnecessary replies to announcement threads

## 4. Posting Guidelines

- Use clear and descriptive subject lines
- Do not send empty or low-effort messages
- Avoid top-posting when replying
- Trim unnecessary quoted text

## 5. Technical Content

- Include logs or error messages when relevant
- Format code properly
- Provide enough context for others to understand your issue

## 6. Spam and Abuse

- No spam or self-promotion
- No unrelated advertisements

Violations may result in moderation or removal from the list.

## 7. Language

- English is the primary language of this list

## 8. Final Notes

This mailing list follows common open source mailing list practices similar to projects like Debian.

Keep it clean, useful, and focused.
